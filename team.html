MICHAEL CHO

President

Michael Cho is the President of Olive Hill Group, and guides the vision and strategy for the firm’s investments. Michael has more than 15 years of experience in the acquisition, repositioning, and operation of commercial properties both domestically and internationally.
Michael is a licensed attorney in the State of California. He graduated from the University of California, Berkeley, and earned his J.D. at Northwestern University.

TIM LEE

Vice President Corp. Development & Legal Affairs

Tim Lee is Vice President,he oversees acquisition and development strategies and drives the firm’s ongoing growth. Previously, he was the co-founder and CEO of tech startup Pogo Linux, Inc., and later served as an attorney for technology startups with Fenwick & West. Tim holds an MBA in Finance from the University of Cambridge, and earned a J.D. at Northwestern University. He is a licensed attorney in the State of California.
