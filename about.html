About

Olive Hill Group LLC is a well-capitalized buyer with the ability to close commercial real estate acquisitions quickly. The company is actively growing its portfolio with an investment strategy that is focused in two areas:
1) creative oce campuses in areas with either value-add opportunites, price appreciation potential, or both;
